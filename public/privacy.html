Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you create a festival wish, we collect:

  • Your name (as provided)
  • Your custom message
  • Uploaded photo and optional audio file
  • Festival type selection
  • IP address (for rate limiting and security)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data solely to:

  • Generate and host your shareable wish link
  • Display your wish to the intended recipient
  • Track view counts for your wish
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting

3. Data Storage

Uploaded images and audio are stored securely via Cloudinary. Wish data is stored in MongoDB. All wishes automatically expire after 365 days and are permanently deleted.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or for the core functionality of the service (e.g., Cloudinary for file hosting).

5. Cookies

FestWish does not use tracking cookies. We may use session storage for form state only.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your wish and associated data at any time by contacting us. All data is automatically deleted after 365 days.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@festwish.com.
